Calamay Run 2025

Be Part of the Historic First-Ever Calamay Run in Jagna, Bohol!

This inaugural event proudly celebrates Calamay, the renowned local delicacy of Jagna, known for its rich, sweet flavor and traditional craftsmanship.

Join us in promoting health, community spirit, and cultural heritage. All proceeds from this event will contribute to the construction of the Ilihan Shrine, a significant landmark in our town.
